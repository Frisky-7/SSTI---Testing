Some of us traders also use some mining software.
Mining software allows you to use your own computer's CPU and GPU to process transactions on the blockchain, giving you some profit per confirmation, also known as hashes. CPU's often will mine in hashes per second (H/s), while GPU's will often mine in megahashes per second (MH/s)
